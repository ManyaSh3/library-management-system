Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I search for books?
A: Yes, the library has an inbuilt search feature that allows you to search for books by both name and section.
Q: Do I get a personalized dashboard?
A: Yes, you get a personalized dashboard with graphs showing section-wise issues, including a bar graph and a donut chart.
Q: In how many days will the librarian accept my book issue request?
A: The librarian will accept your book issue request at the earliest.
Q: What happens if I don't return the book within the time period?
A: If you don't return the book within the specified time period, your access will be revoked, and you won't be able to read that book.
Q: Can I request a book for a certain amount of days?
A: Yes, you can request a book for any number of days between 1 to 7.
Q: Can I download books from the library?
A: Yes, you can download books from the library after paying a nominal fee.
Q: What is the maximum number of days I can issue a book?
A: The maximum number of days to issue a book is 7.
Q: How many books can I issue at max at a time?
A: You can issue up to 5 books at a time.
